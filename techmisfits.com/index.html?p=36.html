Bios

 

TechMisfits is run by three super misfits from around the tech world.

Resno

– The host and king of the misfits.  He has been doing computer geekery since using his IBM Pc Jr, and Commodore 64.  Actually, he never owned one, but he’s been around computers for a while.  Now-a-days he programs, is the keeper of cool.

Integgroll

– That other guy that you hear on the show, you know the smooth one who talks all the time, but never really says anything.  Poor eyesight, burns easily, and two engineer parents; pretty much doomed to computers.

Linc

– He was a gangsta when being a gangsta was cool. He’s a thug from the mean streets of cyberville. Always on the prawl for that latest and greatest technology.
